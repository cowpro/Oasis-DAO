What is Oasis DAO?

Oasis DAO, is a financial management service that makes conservative investments in NFT assets on the Ethereum Blockchain on the behalf of our DAO members who contribute to a liquidity pool and receive vote tokens based on the rarities of their Oasis DAO NFTs. Our customers are able to view the DAOs recent investments/trades propositions from our website dashboard and will have their Oasis DAO NFT viewable on the NFT marketplace Opensea.io.



Our Goal

Oasis DAO is a company created for the Junior Achievement competition at the founder's high school. Our goal is to create a functioning DAO which allows for our NFT holders to benefit financially from the NFT boom and create relationships within our exclusive DAO community. We aim to multiply the DAO's digital asset balance as much as possible while maintaining a conservative approach to possible DAO investments.



Benefits

Purchase of an Oasis DAO NFT allows the holder the following:

  • Access to contribute to DAO liquidity pool

  • Reports on DAO holdings + calls

  • DAO voting rights (varies based on Membership level)

  • Access to community Instagram and Discord


Our Investing Strategy

  • Find a project and join the community

  • Research founders and project outline

  • Determine if project has created natural hype for its drop and decide if the roadmap will keep investors interested

  • Attempt to gain whitelist access to NFT drop by benefiting the community or through other NFT holdings

  • Mint NFT on launch day and sell or hold a little longer according to community and market stability



Team

Our team is comprised of four high-school business and Web3 enthusiasts with various areas of expertise. Together we have come to create a DAO focused on NFT investments. We aim to be as transparent as possible with our NFT holders and only make relaively safe investments.
